# wecheck.ing - Product Summary

## What It Is

A prediction quiz platform where users answer Yes/No questions about upcoming events, compete on leaderboards, and track their prediction accuracy over time.

## Target Audience

- Friends and communities who want to make predictions together
- Groups following sports, entertainment, or any topic with predictable outcomes
- Anyone who enjoys friendly competition and proving they "called it"

## Core Experience

1. **Admin posts a quiz** with 3 Yes/No prediction questions
2. **Users submit answers** before the deadline
3. **After the event**, admin reveals correct answers
4. **Scores update** and leaderboard shows who predicted best

---

## Current Features

- User registration and login
- Quiz creation with deadlines
- Answer submission with countdown timer
- Automatic scoring when answers are revealed
- Season-long leaderboard rankings

## Brand: wecheck.ing

**Tagline:** "We are checking... your predictions"

### The Name Story
Inspired by the iconic Ferrari F1 team radio meme "We are checking" - a phrase that became legendary for its association with dramatic strategy moments in Formula 1. The name captures:

- The anticipation of checking race results
- F1 community inside humor
- The fun of verifying who predicted correctly
